Whitepaper

Introduction:
CPN started as a meme coin but is built with a serious long-term vision: a BTC-backed, community-driven ecosystem. The BTC reserve is permanently locked within the system, and the CPN blockchain ensures transparent, decentralized value coverage.

No Central Company:
Fully decentralized – no freezing, censorship, or control by a central entity. Node operators and stakers maintain the network.

cBTC – Wrapped BTC:
The BTC reserve is represented on chain via cBTC. Stakers unlock rewards through cBTC, while original BTC remain untouchable. This provides secure, auditable rewards without risk to the reserve.

Mining & Staking:
21 Million CPN on the chain must be mined by community nodes. Node operators validate blocks and earn CPN. Stakers earn rewards via cBTC, contributing to the BTC reserve’s growth.

Proof of Value (PoV):
Each CPN is backed by BTC. The system grows sustainably through mining, staking, and the 5% fee dedicated to reserve building and community rewards.

Blockchain Vision

CPN’s vision is to keep BTC in the hands of the community. By creating scarcity and permanently locking BTC into the system, whales cannot easily acquire them. This generates demand for CPN and rewards the community continuously through BTC.

The ecosystem ensures that every participant benefits sustainably: node operators and stakers earn CPN and cBTC rewards while contributing to network security and reserve growth.

FAQ

  • Is CPN a financial product? No.
  • How is the BTC reserve verified? Publicly via explorer.
  • Can I redeem CPN for BTC? Yes, via cBTC on the CPN chain.
